# Monk Timer Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** July 27, 2025

---

I, **Mikail Karadeniz**, as the developer of the "Monk Timer" ("Application") mobile application, take your privacy very seriously. This privacy policy explains how your privacy is protected when you use the Application.

> **My philosophy is simple: I do not collect any personal or non-personal data about my users.**

## 1. Data Collection and Use

The Monk Timer application **does not collect any data from its users.**

- **No Personal Information:** I do not ask for, collect, or store any information that can personally identify you, such as your name, email address, or location.
- **No Usage Data:** I do not track your usage habits, such as how you use the application or which features you run and how often.
- **No Device Information:** I do not collect technical information such as your device model, operating system, or ID.
- **No Server Communication:** The application does not require an internet connection to perform its basic functions and does not send any information to my servers.

> All data you create within the application (for example, timer settings) is **stored locally and exclusively on your own device.** Neither I nor any third party has access to this data. When you delete the application, this data is also permanently deleted from your device.

## 2. Third-Party Services

My application **does not use** any third-party analytics, advertising, or crash reporting services that collect user data. This ensures that the privacy of your data is entirely under your control.

## 3. Data Security

Since I do not collect any data from you, there is no risk of a data leak or breach. Your data resides only on your device and is protected by your device's own security mechanisms.

## 4. Children's Privacy

As my application does not collect data from anyone, it is completely safe for users of all ages. The application does not specifically target children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly or unknowingly collect any information from them.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

If I need to make any changes to this privacy policy in the future (for example, if I add a new feature that requires data collection), I will inform you by updating the policy on this page. However, my current policy is that no data is collected.
